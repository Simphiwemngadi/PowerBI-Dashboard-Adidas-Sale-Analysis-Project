## Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive sales performance dashboard for Adidas**, designed to provide **clear, data-driven insights** into sales, profitability, and regional performance.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor **key performance indicators (KPIs)**, identify top-performing regions, products, and retailers, and analyze sales trends over time.

---

## Objectives
- Track overall sales performance and profitability  
- Analyze sales trends by **month, region, state, product, and retailer**  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming segments  
- Support data-driven business and strategic decisions  

---

## Key KPIs
- **Total Sales:** $885M  
- **Units Sold:** 2M  
- **Operating Profit:** $327M  
- **Average Price per Unit:** $45  
- **Operating Margin:** 42%  

---

## Dashboard Features
### Time-Based Analysis
- Monthly sales trend analysis to identify peak and low sales periods  

### Geographic Insights
- Sales distribution by **U.S. state** (map visualization)  
- Regional contribution breakdown:
  - West  
  - Northeast  
  - Southeast  
  - South  
  - Midwest  

### Product Performance
- Sales by product category, including:
  - Men’s Street Footwear  
  - Women’s Apparel  
  - Men’s Athletic Footwear  
  - Women’s Street Footwear  
  - Men’s & Women’s Apparel  

### Retailer Performance
- Top retailers by total sales:
  - West Gear  
  - Foot Locker  
  - Sports Direct  
  - Kohl’s  
  - Amazon  
  - Walmart  

---

## Interactivity
- **Region slicer** for focused analysis  
- **Invoice date filter** for custom time ranges  
- Dynamic visuals that update based on selections

##  Dataset
- Adidas retail sales transaction data from Kaggle 
- Includes:
  - Invoice dates  
  - Sales revenue  
  - Units sold  
  - Product categories  
  - Retailers  
  - Geographic information  

---

## Key Insights
- Strong overall profitability with a **42% operating margin**
- Sales peak during mid-year months  
- The **West region** contributes the highest share of sales  
- **West Gear and Foot Locker** are the top-performing retailers  
- Men’s footwear categories drive the largest portion of revenue
